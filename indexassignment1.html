MANMOHAN SINGH(26 September 1932-26 December 2024) Prime Minister of India

MANMOHAN SINGH

Manmohan Singh was an Indian politician, economist, academic, and bureaucrat, who served as the 13th prime minister of India from 2004 to 2014.
He was the fourth longest-serving prime minister after Jawaharlal Nehru, Indira Gandhi and Narendra Modi.

here are some notable achievements of the Manmohan Singh (2004-2014):
Indo-US Nuclear Deal:
The government achieved a robust 8.5% GDP growth rate for most of its tenure, making it one of the fastest-growing major economies in the world.
Indo-US Nuclear Deal:
The government signed the Indo-US Civil Nuclear Agreement in 2008, allowing India to access nuclear technology and fuel from the international market.
Special Economic Zones (SEZs):
The government established SEZs to promote exports and attract foreign investment, with the SEZ Act 2005 receiving presidential approval in 2006.
Right to Information (RTI) Act:
The government passed the RTI Act in 2005, providing citizens with the right to access government information and promoting transparency and accountability.
